Our History

Founded in 2023, Mandirigma Automoto Club was built by car lovers who share the same passion for automotive culture.

Nature of the Club

We organize car meets, road trips, car shows, and automotive charity events.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To promote safe driving and unity among car enthusiasts.

Vision: To become one of the most respected car clubs in the region.

About Us

Mandirigma Automoto Club is a student-led community at the University of the East Caloocan united by a shared passion for cars, motorcycles, and automotive culture. We welcome riders, drivers, and enthusiasts of all kinds — bringing together people who value knowledge, camaraderie, and the road. Beyond the machines, we are a community built on respect, responsibility, and genuine connection. We are proud to represent UE Caloocan with unity and the true spirit of a mandirigma.
